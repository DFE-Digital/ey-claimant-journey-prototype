To process your application

We will check the information you have provided with the:

  • further education provider where you currently teach
  • Student Loans Company
  • Teacher's Pension Scheme, if you have a teacher reference number (TRN) and choose to provide it

How we'll pay you

DfE will make the payment directly to you.

To complete the rest of your application, you will need your:

  • National Insurance number
  • bank account details

Student loan deductions

If you are currently paying off a student loan, a deduction from your payment will go towards repaying it.

Income Tax

Your targeted retention incentive payment is taxable income. However DfE will pay any Income Tax and National Insurance contributions due to HMRC for you. This means the payment amount you receive may be lower following tax deductions.

For more details, you can read about payments and deductions for the targeted retention incentive payment (opens in new tab).
