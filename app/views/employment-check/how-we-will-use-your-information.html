To process your application

We will check the information you have provided with the:

  • early years provider where you currently teach
  • Student Loans Company, if you have a student loan

To pay you

DfE will make the payment directly to you.

To complete the rest of your application, you will need your:

  • National Insurance number
  • bank account details

Student loan deductions

If you are currently paying off a student loan, a deduction from your payment will go towards repaying it.

Income Tax

DfE will pay National Insurance and basic rate Income Tax on the recruitment bonus on your behalf. If you earn £50,270 or more a year, you’ll need to pay any Income Tax at the higher rate through PAYE.

For more details, you can read about payments and deductions for the early years financial incentive payment (opens in new tab).
