Early years financial incentive

The early years financial incentive is a new scheme to support nurseries with hiring new practitioners.

It will enable some nurseries to advertise jobs with a £1000 sign-on bonus attached.

The bonus payment is paid to eligible practitioners after they've completed 6 months in their job.

  1. Step 1: Allocation

    The local authority (LA) will have a set number of financial incentives that eligible nurseries can use to recruit permanent staff.

    The nursery works with the LA to obtain these incentive payments, so that they can use them to attract new practitioners.

  2. Step 2: Recruitment

    The nursery advertises an early years practitioner role, with a bonus payment attached.

    Anyone can apply for the role. Applicants do not need to have worked or have experience in a nursery or early years setting to apply.

    Applicants are interviewed. Succesful applicants are told that a claim for the bonus payment will be started on their behalf.

  3. Step 3: Nursery starts claim

    After the practitioner has started the role, the nursery starts a claim on behalf of the practitioner. They do this using an online claim service.

    When they've done this, an email is sent automatically to the practitioner. The email contains a link and claim reference number, and the practitioner is invited to the complete the claim online.

  4. Step 4: Practitioner completes claim

    The practitioner completes the claim using the online claim service. As part of this process, the practitioner will need to sign in to GOV.UK One Login or create an account and prove their identity if they don't already have one.

    When the claim is complete, a confirmation email is sent to the practitioner with the next steps. The nursery also gets an email confirming that the claim is complete.

  5. Step 5: Payment

    After 6 months, the Department for Education will check with the nursery that the practitioner is still working for them.

    If they are, the full bonus payment will be paid into their bank account.
